About Me


I am a Master's Computer Engineering student at the University of California, Irvine. I received a B.S. in Electrical Engineering, with a concentration in Computer and Embedded Systems, at the University of Houston.

For the past three summers, I have been a Solid-State Drive Validation Intern for Intel. In the spring of 2018, I was an intern for the Autopilot Hardware team at Tesla. I was an intern at Microsoft on the Surface team in the summer of 2018.


My interests include embedded systems, firmware, linux kernel development, real-time operating systems and computer architecture. I am currently looking for full-time software developer positions for the Fall 2019.
